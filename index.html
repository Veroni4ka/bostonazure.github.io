Boston Azure User Groups

Serving the Boston technology community since Oct 2009.

  • During "normal" times we operate in-person events in TWO LOCATIONS:
  • Boston Azure
    • In-person events are in the Boston/Cambridge, MA area, usually at Microsoft NERD or MIT.
    • You can review Boston Azure event listings on our Meetup page.
    • Follow Boston Azure @bostonazure on Twitter.
  • North Boston Azure
    • In-person events are in the Burlington, MA area, usually at Microsoft Burlington office.
    • You can review North Boston Azure event listings on our Meetup page.
    • You can follow North Boston Azure @NorthBTownAzure on Twitter.
  • Join our public Slack workspace https://bit.ly/baslack.
  • Subscribe to our YouTube channel
  • Read our Code of Conduct.

While we all deal with the coronvirus/COVID-19 pandemic we are limiting ourselves to virtual/online events.

Please see the above event listing pages to see what we are up to.
